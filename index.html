Warp Wagon

Seconds-Long Space Survival for Apple Watch

Warp Wagon is a lightweight, watch-only space exploration game designed for quick, stealth play sessions. Make fast decisions, manage your crew and energy, and chase a high score.

Inspired by games like The Oregon Trail, Warp Wagon removes the destination and keeps the tension.

Game Modes

  • Main Mission — Endless space survival with global high scores
  • Battle Practice — Learn the systems without risking a run

Availability

Available on the App Store for Apple Watch.
