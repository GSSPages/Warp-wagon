Warp Wagon

Seconds-Long Space Survival for Apple Watch

Warp Wagon is a lightweight, watch-only space exploration game designed for quick, stealth play sessions. Make fast decisions, manage your crew and energy, and chase a high score.

Inspired by games like The Oregon Trail, Warp Wagon removes the destination and keeps the tension.

Game Modes & Features

  • Main Mission — Space survival. Warp to various planets, stars, asteroid fields and other space anomalies. Encounter events that require decisions that can aid or hinder your mission. Among the stars, you may encounter dangerous Space Pirates that you must battle. Good luck, Captain!
  • Battle Practice — Practice battling Space Pirates in waves.
  • Local & Global High Scores — Compete via Game Center
  • Designed for seconds-long play sessions
  • Watch-only, one-handed gameplay
  • No ads, no subscriptions, no micro-transactions
  • Free updates
